## 🖌 Overview  
DaVinci Resolve Advanced is the most complete professional post-production application available, combining a full-featured video editor, the industry's most advanced color grading system, a visual effects compositor, and a professional audio workstation in a single integrated platform.

The Cut and Edit pages deliver a flexible editing experience covering everything from fast assembly cuts to complex multi-layer timelines with speed ramps, fusion composites, and nested timelines.

The Color page is the standard against which all other color grading tools are measured. Node-based grading, HDR wheels, qualifier tools, tracker, and OpenFX plugin support give colorists the precision and depth that major film and television productions demand.

Fusion provides a full node-based compositing environment for visual effects, motion graphics, and title design, integrated directly into the timeline without requiring a round-trip to a separate application.

For colorists, editors, VFX artists, and post-production professionals who want a single application capable of handling every stage of the post workflow at the highest professional standard, DaVinci Resolve Advanced is the definitive platform.
